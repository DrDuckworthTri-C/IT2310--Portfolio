About Me

Educator. Technologist. Innovator.

I am a dynamic educator and healthcare IT professional with a PhD in Information Systems and over 15 years of industry experience transforming complex technical concepts into accessible, career-ready curriculum. My work bridges computer science fundamentals, healthcare informatics, and real-world technology — creating inclusive, hands-on learning environments where students thrive.

Currently serving as Adjunct Faculty and College Credit Plus Preceptor at Cuyahoga Community College (Tri-C), I design and deliver CompTIA Tech+ certification preparatory courses to high school students ages 14–18. I am committed to student success, faculty collaboration, and growing Tri-C's Computer Science pipeline.

Previously, I spent nearly a decade as a Senior IT Clinical Applications Analyst at University Hospitals, leading Epic EHR implementations and mentoring cross-functional technical teams — experience that directly informs my teaching of systems analysis, workflow design, and technical problem-solving.
