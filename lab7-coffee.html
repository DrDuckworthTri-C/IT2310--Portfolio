Dr. Carelicha Duckworth

Founder & Owner, Java Haven

Java Haven was born from a simple belief: great coffee should feel like a warm embrace. Dr. Carelicha Duckworth — educator, technologist, and lifelong coffee enthusiast — founded Java Haven in 2024 with a vision to create a community-centered coffeehouse where every cup tells a story.

Dr. Duckworth holds a PhD in Information Systems and brings the same precision and passion she applies to her academic career to every roast and blend. What started as a single popup location in Cleveland has grown into a beloved neighborhood destination, serving hundreds of loyal guests each week.

  • 2024 — Java Haven opens its doors; signature Latte launches to immediate popularity.
  • 2024 — Launched the signature Haven Blend — a single-origin Ethiopian roast.
  • 2025 — Expanded the menu to include seasonal specials and house-made syrups.
  • 2025 — Partnered with local bakeries and community organizations to support workforce development.
  • 2026 — Celebrating 2 years of community, craft, and caffeination!
